## Mentorship Service

The Mentorship Service helps students receive guidance and support from alumni and professors.

- Students can request alumni to become their mentors.
- Alumni may choose to accept or decline these requests.
- Once connected, mentors and students can communicate privately.
- Professors help ensure that mentorship interactions remain respectful and helpful.
- this service supports students in learning from experienced people in a positive and supportive way.

## Chat Service (1-to-1)

Enables private conversations between two users.

- Creates a chat room between two people
- Stores messages sent between users
- Keeps track of who sent each message and when
- Figures out who the receiver is based on the sender
